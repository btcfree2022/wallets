Escrow Options

If funds are received after this date they will be returned to the buyer and neither side will not be obligated to fulfill any terms of the transaction.
The number of days determines the escrow fee. 1-3 days: 0.40%, 4-9 days: 0.65%, 10-21 days: 0.90%, 22-30 days: 1.05%, 22-30 days: 1.05%, 30+ days: 1.15% + 0.5% per month.
